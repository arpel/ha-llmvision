# Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 15, 2026

### 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how LLM Vision (“we,” “our,” or “us”) handles information in connection with our website (https://llmvision.org) and the LLM Vision Home Assistant integration.

By using the LLM Vision Home Assistant integration, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

---

### 2. Information We Collect
**Website Analytics**

We use Google Analytics to better understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics may collect information such as:
- Pages visited
- Time spent on pages
- Browser type
- Device type
- General geographic region
- Referring websites

This information is aggregated and anonymized. We use it solely to improve our website and user experience. For more information about how Google handles data, please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy.

### 3. Information We Do Not Collect
**No User Accounts**

We do not require user accounts and do not collect personal information such as:
- Names
- Email addresses
- Physical addresses
- Payment information

**No Telemetry in the Home Assistant Integration**

The LLM Vision Home Assistant integration contains no telemetry or tracking mechanisms.
Specifically:
- We do not collect usage data.
- We do not receive any information about how the integration is used.
- We do not see how many users have downloaded the integration.
- We do not see which providers users configure or use.
- We do not have access to user configurations or Home Assistant data.
All processing occurs locally within the user’s Home Assistant environment and according to the configuration they control.

### 4. Data Sharing
Because we do not collect personal data (other than aggregated website analytics via Google Analytics), we do not sell, rent, or share user information with third parties.

### 5. Acceptance of This Policy
By installing or using the LLM Vision Home Assistant integration, you acknowledge and accept this Privacy Policy.

### 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Effective Date” at the top of this document.

### 7. Contact
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact LLM Vision at hello@llmvision.org.
